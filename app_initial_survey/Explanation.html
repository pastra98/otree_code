Description

Intro

Welcome to the experiment!

You are a citizen living in a bigger city somewhere in the world. The city wants to give engaged citizens like you the option to notify them about problems that you encounter in everyday life through a mobile application.

You're using this application today and based on your contributions and those of others, the city you live in will be able to work on your reported issues. The more reports you and your peers contribute, the more the city improves.

Please read the information on this page carefully because your comprehension will be tested.

The setting

You and the other participants may submit certain reports that we have already created for you. Every participant will receive between 4 and 6 effort points. These effort points resemble the amount of effort that you would put into a report on your app, when encountering a problem in your city. You will receive points at the end, based on your individual contribution and based on how much effort other participants put into their submission.

The underlying mechanism of points generation will be the same for everyone. You will play 7 rounds, every round is a clean slate where you can only spend the effort points you are provided with.

The Decision

You can decide to submit between 0 and 4 effort points per submission. Every effort point put into the submission will make the submission clearer for the city, and thus increase the likelihood of the problem being remedied. You have to make this decision without any form of communication with other participants.

The Consequences

You and other participants earn extra points for every submitted report about an issue. A group of 4 players contributes to a shared pot that will be distributed. All the points that you chose not to submit will be added together with those extra points from the shared pot. This is called your "payout" and it is calculated in the following way:

payout = Initial effort points - Your contribution + (total contribution by other participants / 4) * 1.5)

This means that every effort point spent on good reports will be worth 50% more once distributed among the participants.

After you and the other participants have made your decision, you will be presented with your payout, as well as some additional information.

Process

(1) After the comprehension questions, we will ask you to rank yourself from 1 to 10 based on your monthly available income.

(2) Afterwards you will be presented with an issue found in your city, to which you can dedicate effort points from 0 (no submission) to 4 effort points (full submission). Based on your spending, the quality of the submission will be higher and thus make the issue clearer to the city.

Questions

Only one answer is correct.
